Paradox Foundry · a time-loop automation puzzle

You control one worker for a fixed-length time loop. When the loop ends, your recorded moves become a permanent echo that replays — forever, in lock-step — beside the next you. Bootstrap an entire factory out of cooperating copies of your past selves… without letting them collide or starve. Every conflict is a paradox.
